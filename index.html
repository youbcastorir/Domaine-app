The Pricing Formula

Our proprietary equation combines six key domain value indicators into a single, precise market estimate.

P = ((K × B × S × E × R) / L0.8) + log(A+1) × M
P = Final Domain Price
K = Keyword Strength
B = Brandability Score
S = Search Volume Score
E = TLD Extension Multiplier
R = Rarity Factor
L = Domain Length
A = Domain Age (years)
M = Market Trend Multiplier
